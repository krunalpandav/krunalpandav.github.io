Privacy Policy

Diamond Work Book · Last updated: 31 May 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Diamond Work Book ("the app", "we", "us") handles your information. The app helps diamond workers record their daily work, working days, and withdrawals. By using the app you agree to this policy.

1. Information you enter

All work data you create in the app — daily work records, working days, and withdrawal entries — is stored only on your device. We do not upload, transmit, sync, or have any access to this data. The app does not require you to create an account or provide any personal details such as your name, phone number, or email.

2. Information collected automatically (advertising)

The app displays advertisements served by Google AdMob. To deliver ads, Google and its partners may collect and process certain information, including your device's advertising identifier, IP address, general device information, and app-usage interactions. This processing is governed by Google's own policies:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • How Google uses information from sites or apps that use its services

You can reset or limit ad personalization at any time from your device's settings (Settings → Google → Ads).

3. Permissions the app uses

  • Internet & network state — required only to load advertisements.
  • Notifications — used to send an optional daily reminder. You can disable this in your device's notification settings.
  • Run at startup — used to reschedule the daily reminder after the device restarts.

4. Children's privacy

The app is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

5. Data retention and deletion

Because your work data lives only on your device, uninstalling the app permanently deletes all of it. You may also clear the app's data from your device settings at any time.

6. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
