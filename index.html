Hi, I'm Krunal — an Android developer from Gujarat, India.

I've been building Android apps professionally since January 2021 as the lead developer on Strategy X, a production client app I've shipped and maintained for over 5 years. Across that journey I've gained 6+ years of hands-on engineering experience.

My focus is on writing clean, testable Kotlin, designing offline-first experiences with Realm and Room, and architecting modular apps with MVVM + Dagger Hilt. I've also built REST APIs in Laravel and Core PHP to back my mobile work — so I can own a feature end-to-end.

Outside of office work, I ship my own products — Diamond Work Book and Card Vault — that solve real problems for real users with a strong privacy-first stance.
